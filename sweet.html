Sweet Recipes

ALthough I have a dedicated savory palette, the thrill of baking the perfect scone, cake, cookie, or pie is unbeatbale. Sweet baking it a delicate balance of sugar and infused flavors.

To bake the perfect pie is nearly impossible. Factors such as actual oven temperature, humidity levels in the air, and quality of ingredients can greatly alter the outcome of a bake. Sweet recipes can be finicky and I don't want to take on the responsibility of any failed attempts in the kitchen. Instead, I will provide links to three of my favorite summer sweet bakes!
